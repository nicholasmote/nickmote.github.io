Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 1 Jar of Jelly
  • 1 Jar of Peanut Butter
  • A Butter Knife
  • A Sharp Knife

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on plate.
  2. With butter knife, spread layer of jelly on one slice of bread
  3. Spread layer of peanut butter on other slice of bread.
  4. Carefully place two slices of bread on top each other.
  5. With a sharp knife, carefully trim crust from bread.
  6. Cut sandwich diagonally
  7. Enjoy the PB&J!
